# IntervueX AI – Your Brutally Honest Interviewer 🎯

> **"I’ll take your interview before companies do."**

## 💡 The Problem
A major issue among early-stage professionals and freshers is the **lack of real interview practice**. Traditional mock interviews are expensive, hard to schedule, or often sugarcoated by friends. Students need a way to be rigorously tested *before* they sit in front of real companies.

## 🚀 The Solution: IntervueX AI
IntervueX is a premium, purpose-built AI product that simulates real technical interviews for freshers. 
It behaves like a strict but helpful interviewer from a top tech company. This isn't your generic AI chatbot—it's a high-stakes, pressure-tested mock interview environment with actionable feedback.

---

## ✨ Features
1. **Interactive Interview Mode:** Get asked relevant technical, behavioral, or system design questions one by one.
2. **Brutal Mode Toggle:** 
   - 🟢 **Normal Mode:** Supportive, helpful, guides you along.
   - 🔴 **Brutal Mode:** Direct, honest, tough feedback. No sugarcoating.
3. **Live Scorecard:** After 3-5 questions, automatically generate an actionable scorecard showing:
   - Your Score (0-10)
   - Strengths
   - Weaknesses
   - Actionable improvement tips
4. **Premium UI/UX:** Dark theme, glassmorphism, micro-interactions, smooth gradient accents, and Framer Motion animations to make it feel like a polished, top-tier startup product.

---

## 💻 Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4, Custom CSS Variables (Glassmorphism, gradients)
- **Animations:** Framer Motion
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Architecture:** Responsive 3-panel layout (Sidebar, Chat Area, Insight Panel)
- **Deployment:** Optimized for Vercel deployment

---

## 🏃 Demo Flow
1. **Landing Page:** User logs onto a premium dark-themed hero section.
2. **Start Interview:** User navigates to the interview dashboard.
3. **Role Selection:** User informs the AI about the role (SDE, Data Analyst, etc.).
4. **Q&A Session:** The AI asks the first question. The user responds typing into the input. The AI responds contextually, offering feedback on the spot based on the selected mode (Normal/Brutal).
5. **Score & Feedback:** After enough questions, the right panel automatically reveals the user's score out of 10, along with strengths and tips.
